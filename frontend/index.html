About Our Vitamin Deficiency Detection App

Our app is designed to help you identify and manage vitamin deficiencies effectively. The app also offers insights into dermatologist findings, a chatbot for health-related queries, and detailed documentation on deficiencies. Embark on your journey toward a healthier life today!
